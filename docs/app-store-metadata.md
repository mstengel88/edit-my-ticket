## Description

Ticket Creator is a field-ready ticket management app built for teams that need to create, edit, review, and share scale-ticket information quickly.

Use Ticket Creator to:

- Create and manage scale tickets from iPhone or iPad
- Edit customer, product, truck, and delivery details
- Preview printable ticket layouts before sending
- Email tickets and reports to customers
- Review totals and activity through built-in reporting tools
- Manage customer and product records in one workflow

Ticket Creator is designed for internal operations teams that need a clean, fast workflow for day-to-day ticket handling.
